Accomodation

Hotels

Starred

Marriot Hotel

Experience Rwanda's capital from the 5-Star Kigali Marriott Hotel, conveniently located near the Nyarugenge financial district and the Kigali Convention Center.

Serena Hotel

The Hotel is situated only 10 km from Kigali International Airport. Ideally located on one of Kigali's most charming boulevards, the 5-star Kigali Serena Hotel is built around a polished granite atrium.

Radisson Blue Hotel

Radisson Blu Hotel has an outdoor swimming pool, garden, a bar and shared lounge in Kigali.

The Radisson Blu Hotel is just 5 kilometers from the bustling city center and Kigali International Airport.

City Blue Epic Hotel

City Blue Epic Hotel is located in the Eastern Province District of Nyagatare in Rwanda 149 KM from Kigali international airport, and just 50 KM from the Rwanda-Uganda border point of Kagitumba.

Resorts

Palm Beach Resort

The Palm Beach Resort offers rooms with air conditioning in Gisenyi. The property is within a 19-minute walk of Viewpoint, The Congo Nile Trail Path One and Primus Brewery. Each room is equipped with a terrace with lake views and free WiFi.

Kivu Paradis Resort

Located in Nyamyumba next to National Breweries, Kivu Paradis Resort features a garden and free WiFi. With a bar, the property also features a restaurant. The property offers a children's playground.

Speaking Afrikaans and English, staff will be happy to provide guests with practical advice on the area at the reception.

Nirvana Heights Resort

A charming lake front resort with 18 hotel rooms, all en-suite rooms with a balcony overlooking the lake Kivu. All of our suites boast majestic views and offer a chic, comfortable and luxurious space for our guests.
