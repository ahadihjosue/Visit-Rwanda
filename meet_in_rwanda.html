Kigali Convention Centre

About KCC

Plan Your Event

The landmark Kigali Convention Centre (KCC) is the first and largest Convention Centre in the region, and the leading destination for events in Rwanda. Its unique design integrates all the facilities and functional technology with a clear reference to the culture and traditions of Africa.

Modern & Professional Conferences in Kigali

As the leading event and conference centre in Kigali, the KCC features state-of-the-art technology, personalised service and professional facilitation to ensure event success.

Meeting Venues

Meeting venues at the KCC can easily be combined and configured to offer unrivaled flexibility and scale for all sizes of events in Rwanda.

Around The City

Restaurants

Soko - Breakfast Buffet

Open for breakfast, lunch and dinner, this modern restaurant in Kigali, Rwanda serves global cuisine in an airy, atrium-styled atmosphere or on a delightful, al fresco terrace. Order a la carte dishes or select your meal from the lavish buffet choices.

Poivre Noire

Poivre Noir is a convivial place, dedicated to the love of good food. Whether you are a gourmet or an epicurean, whether you enjoy classic bistro cuisine or you are simply curious and open to culinary discoveries.

The Hut

The Hut Restaurant & Boutique Hotel is located at close proximity to the prestigious Kigali convention centre. The ambience and really tasty food made for a welcome meal on the way home after a long day.
